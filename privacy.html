Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

The Sukhmani Sahib application is built as a free, open-source Progressive Web App (PWA) intended for community Sewa. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

Information Collection and Use

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data, user identifier data, or analytical information. Everything runs entirely on your local device.

Cookies and Local Storage

The app only utilizes standard browser caching and Service Workers to allow offline access to the text and audio features. No tracking cookies are used.

Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, feel free to open an issue on the GitHub repository.
